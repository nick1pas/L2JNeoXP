At your command a clan will be dissolved.
After making a request to dissolve a clan, you can cancel that request within one week by submitting a request to recover the clan. However, you cannot make another request to dissolve the clan again within seven days after the cancellation.
After requesting to dissove a clan, the clan's leader will lose Exp equivalent to one death and the clan leader cannot create another clan for 10 days after dissoving his clan.
Dissolved clans may be recovered by requesting recovery within one week.
